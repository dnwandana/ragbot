# Members & Roles

<p class="lede">Workspaces are shared spaces. You invite people in, and each person gets a <strong>role</strong> that decides what they can do — from running the whole workspace to simply reading and chatting.</p>

## Inviting members

If you're an Owner or Admin, open **Workspace settings › Members** and choose **Invite people**. Enter the person's email and pick the role they should start with. They'll get an invitation; once they accept, they appear in your members list and can work in the workspace. Until then, they show in a **pending invitations** section below the member list.

<MembersTableMock />

You can change someone's role or remove them at any time from the same list. A member only ever has access to **this** workspace — inviting someone here never touches your other workspaces.

## The roles

RAGBot keeps roles deliberately simple. Most people are **Editors**; a couple of trusted people are **Admins**; anyone who only needs to read gets **Viewer**.

- **Owner / Admin** — full control. Manage members and roles, change settings, and create, edit, or delete any dataset or agent. The Owner is whoever created the workspace; Admins have the same day-to-day powers.
- **Editor** — the everyday contributor. Upload documents, build datasets and agents, and chat freely. Editors can't manage other people or delete shared resources.
- **Viewer** — read and chat only. Viewers can explore datasets and agents and ask questions, but can't change anything.
